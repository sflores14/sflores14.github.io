Research · Mentoring · Advocacy

Hi, I’m Saul Flores.

I’m a Biochemistry PhD candidate at the University of Maryland, College Park (advanced to candidacy in May 2025). My dissertation research focuses on exopolysaccharide pathways in bacterial biofilms, combining structural biology, membrane protein biochemistry, and computation to connect molecular mechanism to emergent biofilm properties.
