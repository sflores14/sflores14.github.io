Research · Mentoring · Advocacy

Hi, I’m Saul Flores.

I’m a Biochemistry PhD candidate at the University of Maryland, College Park. My dissertation research focuses on exopolysaccharide pathways in bacterial biofilms, combining structural biology, membrane protein biochemistry, and computation to connect molecular mechanism to emergent biofilm properties. I also mentor students through La Familia Peer Mentoring and InspiraSTEM and contribute to science policy initiatives through SNAP and professional societies.
