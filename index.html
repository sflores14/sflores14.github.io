About

Academic profile, research values, and current focus.

I am a researcher who studies how people learn, collaborate, and build with emerging technologies. My work blends quantitative methods with qualitative insights to design tools that are equitable, creative, and empowering.

I am currently exploring how AI can augment scientific workflows, with a focus on transparency, ethics, and reproducible systems. I also collaborate on interdisciplinary projects connecting computer science, education, and design.
